Karisalankanni(Eclipta prostrata)



Karisalankanni is a potent medicinal herb widely used in Ayurvedic and traditional medicine. It is renowned for its liver-protective properties, helping to detoxify and rejuvenate liver function. Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, Karisalankanni supports healthy hair growth, improves skin health, and promotes wound healing. Its benefits extend to treating respiratory ailments and enhancing overall vitality. As a natural remedy, it is valued for its wide range of therapeutic applications.

Nilavembu (Andrographis paniculata)



Nilavembu is a medicinal herb renowned for its numerous health benefits. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine, it is celebrated for its anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and immune-boosting properties. Nilavembu is particularly effective in managing fevers, respiratory infections, and digestive disorders. Its rich antioxidant content helps combat oxidative stress, promoting overall wellness. Additionally, it is often used as a natural remedy to enhance liver function and support detoxification.

Pudina (Mentha)



Pudina, commonly known as mint (), is a fragrant herb widely valued for its medicinal properties. Renowned for its ability to aid digestion, it can alleviate symptoms of indigestion and bloating. Pudina is also known for its cooling effects, making it effective in relieving headaches and reducing stress. Vishnukranta (Clerodendrum serratum)



Vishnukranta is a revered medicinal plant in traditional Indian medicine. It is praised for its anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antimicrobial properties, making it effective in treating various ailments. This herb is commonly used to alleviate respiratory issues, such as coughs and bronchitis, and is known for its benefits in managing fever and joint pain. Vishnukranta is also recognized for its potential in promoting digestive health and enhancing liver function. With its rich array of therapeutic compounds, this plant serves as a valuable ally in holistic health practices.

Sathavari (Asparagus racemosus)



Sathavari is a powerful herb widely used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine. Renowned for its adaptogenic properties, it helps the body cope with stress and promotes hormonal balance, particularly in women. Sathavari is celebrated for its potential to enhance reproductive health, boost lactation in nursing mothers, and improve fertility. Additionally, its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties support overall health by promoting a strong immune system. This versatile plant is also used to improve digestive health and nourish the body's tissues, making it a valuable addition to herbal remedies.

Kadukkai (Terminalia chebula)



Kadukkai is a revered medicinal plant in traditional Ayurvedic and Siddha medicine. Avaram Poo(Cassia auriculata)

Avaram Poo is a medicinal plant widely recognized for its therapeutic benefits. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic and Siddha medicine, it is praised for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
